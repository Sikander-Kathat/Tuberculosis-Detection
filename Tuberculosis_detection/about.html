About Us

Introducing Tuberculosis Detection AI, an innovative online platform revolutionizing tuberculosis diagnosis through the integration of advanced artificial intelligence (AI) models. This website offers a seamless user experience, enabling healthcare professionals and individuals to upload chest X-ray images for rapid analysis. Leveraging state-of-the-art AI algorithms, including DenseNet-121 and LeNet-5, Tuberculosis Detection AI delivers fast and accurate tuberculosis detection, providing instant feedback on the likelihood of infection and accompanying insights. By facilitating early detection and intervention, Tuberculosis Detection AI aims to improve patient outcomes and public health while optimizing healthcare resource utilization.
